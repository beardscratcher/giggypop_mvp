About GiggyPOP

Few people like to go to gigs alone. But organising with friends to buy together can be a hassle. And convincing friends to take your spare tickets can be a chore. GIGGYPOP helps you effectively pitch your gig to your friends, and as soon as a friend commits to joining, we will buy you both tickets. This way, you will always be certain of having a committed friend and a gig ticket.

GIGGYPOP came out of Startup Weekend x Hyper Island by music fans that love going to gigs but think ticket buying could be smarter. Judges from Techstars, Sapient Nitro & Faber Ventures awarded GIGGYPOP first place.
